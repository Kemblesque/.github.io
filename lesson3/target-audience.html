Target Audience:

Who is the audience for Weather Wonder? Who will utilize this website?

Weather Wonder will be utilized by a variety of people who want daily updates on the weather

Who?

Anyone who wants to know what the daily weather will be.

Age Group

Ages 5 to 99 will use the site

Income

All income ranges will use the site

Education

Anyone who knows how to read the weather in Fahrenheit

Personas

Persona 1:

Mike Smith

65 years old

Outdoor enthusiast

Spends time outdoors fishing, hiking, and enjoying sunny days

Persona 2:

Hannah Jones

21 years old

College Student

Spends time outdoors to workout and refresh her mind from the stresses of school

Scenarios

Scenario #1

Will the website be user friendly?

Yes, this website will be designed to accomidate all screen types, from mobile to desktop allowing a friendly user experience.

Scenario #2

Will the weather update often?

The weather will be listed weekly with any changes being updated hourly, for changing weather conditions.

Scenario #3

How will this site be better than other weather sites?

This site is local with a focus on the weather in the areas that matter most to you, your hometown and surrounding areas.

Scenario #4

Plan on fishing this weekend?

With updated weather you will know exactly what to be prepared for on your trip.

Scenario #5

Can I take this site with me?

Our mobile site will provide all the information that our desktop site does, and it fits in your pocket.
