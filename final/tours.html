SCOOTS Top Tour Recommendations

Chichen Itza Mayan Ruins

Our most popular tour by far, but do not go into it lightly. Rent one of our Jeeps and prepare for an adventure you will not forget.

At just over 2 hours one way this trip will take up your entire day, but it will not regret it if you go prepared. We recommend hiring a guide to show you around and teach the history of this beautiful area.

With all of our tour recommendations we suggest taking snacks, water and a change of clothes. You will want and need all of these things on this trip.

Sian Ka'an Biosphere Reserve

Rent one of our Jeeps and take the short drive to the Sain Ka'an Biosphere Reserve. The largest protected area in the Mexican Caribbean, Sian Ka'an is home to thousands of species of flora and fauna.

Sian Ka'an is home to numerous outdoor activities, perfect for the outdoor enthousiast. Snorkeling, birdwatching, and fly fishing are just a few of the activities offered at this beautiful reserve.

Pack a lunch and make a day of this trip. you will not regret it.

When renting your Jeep mention that you are wanting to travel to Sian Ka'an and we will provide maps and directions. At just over an hour away from Playa del Carmen it is a perfect day trip.

The Beaches of Cozumel

Rent one of our many scooters and explore the island beaches of Cozumel.

With 13 different Beaches in Cozumel you're sure to find one that is just right for you. Spend the day exploring the island beaches and taking in all the sun and ocean waves.

The perfect way to spend a few hours or a whole day.

For beach recommendations let us know and we can point out our favorites and directions on how to get there.

San Gervasio Mayan Archaeological Site

Another trip that can be a few hours or a whole day is a visit to the San Gervasio Mayan Archaeological Site.

Rent a scooter and take the short trip to this site and enjoy exploring these mayan ruins.

You can explore this area on your own, but we always recommend a guide to help explain the history, and show you the best sites.

No matter how long of a trip you plan, always come prepared with water and snacks.
